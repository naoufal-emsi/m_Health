M-HEALTH APP PROJECT EXPLANATION

## What is it?
A mobile health app that connects patients with their doctors and medical staff.

## Who uses it?
- **Patients** - Main users who want to manage their health
- **Doctors** - Medical professionals treating patients  
- **Admin** - System manager who controls everything
- **Medical Secretary** - Helps schedule appointments and manage paperwork

## What can patients do?
- Create account and login
- View their medical records and test results
- Book, change, or cancel doctor appointments
- Request prescription refills
- Send messages to their doctor
- Get notifications about treatments and appointments

# What can doctors do?
- Login to secure account
- Access patient medical files and history
- View their appointment schedule
- Send/receive messages with patients
- Review test results and add notes

## What can admin do?
- Create and manage all user accounts
- Set permissions for different users
- Manage patient database
- Keep system secure and running

## What can medical secretary do?
- Schedule and manage appointments
- Access patient contact info (not medical details)
- Update patient files when doctor requests
- Check doctor schedules
- Pass urgent messages to doctors

## Technical details:
- Built for Android phones
- Coded in Java
- Made using Android Studio

**Bottom line**: It's like having a digital bridge between patients and their healthcare team, making medical care more convenient and organized.
